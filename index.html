Delete Your Rightfile Account

Rightfile allows users to request deletion of their account and associated personal data at any time.

How to request account deletion

  1. Send an email to josephchan@rightfile.beauty
  2. Use the subject line: Delete My Rightfile Account
  3. Include the email address used to register your Rightfile account

What data will be deleted

  • User account information (email address)
  • User profile data associated with the account
  • App-related data linked to the user account

Data retention

Some data may be retained temporarily if required by law, security, or fraud prevention, and will be deleted once no longer required.
